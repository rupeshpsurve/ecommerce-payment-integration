## 📌 Purpose

This document defines the end-to-end payment lifecycle requirements for Euronics 
International Ltd's e-commerce platform expansion across 8 European markets.

Requirements were gathered through discovery workshops with ecommerce, finance, and 
customer service teams, and translated into BPMN swimlane diagrams covering 6 key 
payment scenarios.
